OUR STORY -


Our incredible rare beans come from humble beginning sin Yemen, where decades of political turmoil once forced local farmers to start growing khat, a narcotic native to the Arabian Penisula.

The Dawoodi Bohra community changed this, removing all the khat plants and replacing them with coffee, bringing this humble brew back to its roots.
